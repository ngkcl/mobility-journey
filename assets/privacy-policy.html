Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2025

Mobility Journey ("the App") is developed by Mandel AI, Inc. ("we", "us", "our"). Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

a) Apple Health Data (HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, the App reads and writes the following HealthKit data types:

  • Read: Sleep analysis, heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), step count, active energy burned, and workout sessions.
  • Write: Completed mobility workout sessions and exercise minutes.

This data is used solely to personalize your exercise plans, track recovery, and provide insights into your mobility progress. HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, used for advertising, or sold.

b) Photos & Videos

You may optionally capture progress photos or videos using the App. These are:

  • Stored securely in your private Supabase storage bucket.
  • Accessible only to your authenticated account.
  • Used for posture analysis and progress tracking within the App.

c) Camera & Motion Data

  • Camera: Used on-device for real-time posture analysis. Camera frames are processed locally and are not transmitted to any server.
  • AirPods Motion: Head motion data from AirPods Pro is processed on-device to monitor posture. This data is not stored or transmitted.

d) AI Analysis

When you request an AI-powered assessment, your anonymized posture data or exercise descriptions may be sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. This data:

  • Does not include your name, email, or personally identifiable information.
  • Is not retained by Anthropic beyond the API request per their data policy.
  • Is used solely to generate your personalized mobility recommendations.

e) Account Data

We store minimal account information (email address) via Supabase Authentication for login purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate personalized corrective exercise plans based on your health metrics and posture data.
  • Track your mobility progress over time.
  • Provide real-time posture feedback.
  • Improve the App's exercise recommendations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data is shared only with:

  • Supabase — for secure data storage and authentication (SOC 2 Type II compliant).
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — for AI-powered exercise analysis (anonymized data only).

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+.
  • Data at rest is encrypted in Supabase's SOC 2 compliant infrastructure.
  • HealthKit data remains on your device and in Apple's secure HealthKit store.
  • We use row-level security (RLS) to ensure you can only access your own data.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the App.
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke HealthKit, camera, or photo access in iOS Settings at any time.
  • Export: Your workout data synced to Apple Health is always available through the Health app.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or via email.
